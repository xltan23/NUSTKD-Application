Our Head Coach

"I started Taekwondo at the age of 9 and never left it since. My Father was the one who encouraged me to take up this sport and he became both my
mentor and personal coach. He trained me to be one of the best fighters in Singapore during the late 80s and early 90s. I participated in the 1987,
1989 & 1993 SEA Games of which I won Silver and Gold medals in 1989 and 1993 respectively. I also participated in multiple local competitions
sanctioned by STF and won gold medals for my category. My passion for the sport will never die and my motto is to keep training and stay mentally
strong and fit."
